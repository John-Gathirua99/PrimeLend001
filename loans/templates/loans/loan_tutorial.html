Step 01 — Account
Create Your Account
Register with your name, email and a secure password. You'll receive a verification OTP to confirm your email.
  • Go to primelend.onrender.com and click Register
  • Enter your full name as it appears on your National ID
  • Provide a valid email address — you'll receive OTP codes here
  • Create a strong password (8+ characters)
  • Check your email for the OTP code and enter it to verify
  • Complete your profile — add phone number and employment details
💡 Use your real name exactly as it appears on your Kenyan National ID. This helps with automatic identity verification later.

Step 02 — Application
Fill Loan Application
Provide your personal and financial details. Our AI will use this to calculate how much you qualify for.
  • Click Apply for Loan from your dashboard
  • Enter your age and National ID number
  • Select your employment status (Employed, Self-employed, Business, etc.)
  • Enter your monthly income in KES — be accurate for best results
  • Choose your preferred repayment period (1–36 months)
  • Select your credit history rating
  • Write a clear reason for the loan
  • Upload a clear flat photo of your National ID (front and back)
⚠️ Important: Place your ID flat on a dark surface with good lighting. Do NOT hold the ID in your hand — this causes verification failure.
📸 ID photo tips: All 4 corners visible · No glare · Good lighting · High resolution · Flat surface

Step 03 — Identity
Complete Face KYC
Take a live selfie so our AI can verify your face matches the photo on your ID document. This keeps your account secure.
  • Allow camera access when prompted by your browser
  • Wait for the liveness challenge to appear (e.g. "Blink twice slowly")
  • Complete the challenge — this proves you are a live person
  • Once the circle turns green, press Take Selfie
  • Look directly at the camera — no sunglasses, good lighting
  • Wait for the AI to compare your face with your ID (30–60 seconds)
  • If verified ✅ — proceed. If failed ❌ — retake in better lighting
🚫 Do NOT submit a photo of a wall, another person, or a printed photo. The system will detect and reject it.
💡 Best selfie conditions: Face directly in the oval guide · Even natural light from front · No backlighting · Remove glasses if possible
4
Step 04 — Decision
Get AI Decision
Our AI model analyzes your application and gives an instant credit decision — approved, under review, or rejected.
  • After KYC, you are redirected to your loan result page
  • Approved ✓ — you qualify! Proceed to accept terms
  • Under Review — admin will verify manually within 24 hours
  • Rejected ✗ — reapply after improving your credit profile
  • Check your qualified amount — may differ from requested amount
  • Review the interest rate assigned based on your credit score
📊 Your credit score improves with each on-time repayment. Higher scores unlock lower interest rates and higher loan limits.

Step 05 — Terms & Fee
Accept Terms & Pay Fee
Read and accept the loan terms, then pay the processing fee via M-Pesa to activate your loan.
  • Read the loan terms carefully — total repayable amount, interest, period
  • Check the checkbox confirming you agree to the terms
  • Click Accept Terms & Continue
  • You'll see the processing fee (2% of loan amount, min KES 500)
  • Click Pay via M-Pesa — STK push sent to your phone
  • Enter your M-Pesa PIN on your phone to complete payment
  • Wait for confirmation — your loan moves to disbursement queue
📱 Make sure your phone number on your profile matches your M-Pesa registered number. The STK push goes to the number on your profile.
⚠️ Processing fee is non-refundable if you cancel after payment. Only pay when you are ready to proceed.
6
Step 06 — Funds
Receive & Repay
Funds are credited to your PrimeLend wallet. Repay on time to build your trust tier and access higher loan limits.
  • Admin disburses funds to your PrimeLend wallet
  • You receive an in-app notification when funds are credited
  • Go to My Wallet to see your balance
  • To repay — click Repay (Wallet) to deduct from wallet balance
  • Or click M-Pesa to pay directly from your phone
  • Repay on time to upgrade your trust tier and get better rates
  • Download your loan statement PDF from wallet history
🏆 Trust tiers: NEW → BRONZE → SILVER → GOLD → PLATINUM. Each upgrade unlocks higher loan limits (up to KES 500,000) and lower interest rates.
