Important Notice

Please read these terms carefully before applying for a loan. By submitting your application, you agree to be bound by these conditions.

01.Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for a loan through PrimeLend AI, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be a citizen or legal resident of Kenya.
  • Be at least 18 years of age.
  • Possess a valid National Identification Card.
  • Have an active M-PESA mobile money account registered in your own name.
  • Provide accurate and truthful information regarding your employment and income.

02.Loan Terms & Interest

PrimeLend AI offers loans based on your creditworthiness. The specific terms of your loan will be displayed to you prior to acceptance:

Interest Rate

Variable rates calculated based on risk profile. The Annual Percentage Rate (APR) will be disclosed in your loan offer.

Processing Fees

A one-time non-refundable processing fee may be deducted from the loan amount or charged separately.

PrimeLend AI reserves the right to adjust interest rates for future loans based on market conditions and borrower repayment behavior.

03.Repayment Policy

Repayments are facilitated exclusively through M-PESA. By accepting these terms, you authorize PrimeLend AI to:

  1. Send STK Push prompts to your registered phone number for payment collection.
  2. Automatically deduct repayment amounts if you have authorized auto-debit features.
  3. Apply late payment penalties if payment is not received by the due date.

04.AI Credit Assessment

Algorithmic Decision Making

We use artificial intelligence to analyze your creditworthiness. This includes analyzing your transaction history, device metadata, and financial behavior. You have the right to request an explanation regarding any adverse decision made by our AI system.

05.Default & Collections

An "Event of Default" occurs if you fail to pay any sum due on the due date. In such events:

  • A late payment penalty of 5% per month may be applied to the outstanding balance.
  • Your details may be reported to the Credit Reference Bureau (CRB).
  • We may engage third-party debt collection agencies to recover the debt at your cost.

06.Data Protection & Privacy

We are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Data Protection Act.

We collect your ID number, phone number, location data, and financial transaction history solely for the purpose of credit assessment and loan disbursement. We do not sell your data to third parties.
